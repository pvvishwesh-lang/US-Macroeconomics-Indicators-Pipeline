Add the following to .streamlit/secrets.toml:

Your Databricks Token: settings->developer->generate new token

Your Databricks HTTP PATH: Compute->your compute engine->connection details

Your Databricks Host: Found in the URL of browser->https://<id>.cloud.databricks.com/

Your Job ID: Jobs & pipelines->your pipeline->https://<id>.cloud.databricks.com/jobs/<jobid>

Your Dashboard URL: Dashboards->Your dashboard->Share->Embed Dashboard->iframe->Copy from src
